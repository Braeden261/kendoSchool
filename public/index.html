Welcome to Kendō Gakkō, a multi-user, multi-device experience. Learn basic Kendo sword techniques and their associated Japanese names, and put your memory skills to the test in this Simon Says-style game.

Do you have what it takes to graduate from Kendo School?





PLAYER ONE: THE MASTER (MOBILE)

As the Master, you are responsible for providing the Student with a sequence of actions to perform on the training dummy.


define the sequence

send it to the student

watch the student in VR





PLAYER TWO: THE STUDENT (VR)

As the Student, you are responsible for performing the sequence of actions provided to you by the Master.


pick up the shinai

receive sequence from master

execute the sequence
